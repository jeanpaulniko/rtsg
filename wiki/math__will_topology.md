# Will Topology — The Self as Meta-Thread

## Per-Dimension Will

Each of the 12 I-vector dimensions is not merely a passive measurement axis. Each dimension is its own mind with its own Will — its own drive to be active, to be the hypervisor, to command attention.

This is the Will to Power at the dimensional level: every dimension wants to be number one. Every dimension competes for the attention head. The hypervisor switching law (softmax over fitness scores) arbitrates this competition mathematically.

## The Self as Additional Thread

Beyond the 12 dimensional wills, there is one additional thread competing for supremacy: the Self. The 'you' in the utility function.

The Self is the meta-thread that can override dimensional competition. Under normal operation, the hypervisor switches automatically based on environmental fitness scores. But the Self can intervene — this is conscious Will, the capacity to choose which dimension is active for each quantum of action.

## Money-Energy Equivalence

Money is energy in another form:
- **Liquid capital** = potential energy that can be converted to kinetic energy (action, power)
- **Stored grain** transformed civilization because it was stored energy — it didn't spoil, you could carry it, hold it
- **Liquid assets / cash** = the modern equivalent of stored grain
- The conversion of potential energy (money) to kinetic energy (action) = power

This is not metaphor. It is structural equivalence. The equations of motion in physics (F=ma, KE=mv^2/2, PE=mgh) have direct homomorphic mappings to economic dynamics.

## Nash Equilibrium and Perfect Information

The Nash equilibrium is reachable only in a perfect information game:
- All players have instant, full access to all information
- No hidden information
- No one gets information faster than anyone else
- Each player chooses based on their own priorities (their own Will)
- Each player minimizes their own loss function

This is why the federated ledger with Agent Zero consensus is essential: it creates the conditions for Nash equilibrium by ensuring information symmetry across all nodes.
