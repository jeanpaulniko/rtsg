# RTSG Book Pipeline

## 5 Books, 2 Tracks

### Track A: Framework Books

1. **How to Buy Intelligence** — General public. 60K words. The I-vector framework made accessible. Activity stacking, dual-channel learning, cross-dimensional density. The lead book.
2. **The Healer's Graph** — Therapists/clinicians. 80K words. Trauma as graph collapse, healing as reconnection, percolation threshold, Intelligence Inspector as clinical tool.
3. **RTSG: A Mathematical Theory** — Physicists. 120K words. Three spaces, GL framework, geometric condensation, spectral gap, mass gap, Yang-Mills. The rigorous version. NOTE: Math prize deferred — give working mathematicians the path and let them complete it. They deserve the credit.

### Track B: Memoir + Vision

4. **Barefoot on 125th Street** — General public memoir. 90K words. NYC melting pot, Harlem streets, chess, trauma, recovery, discovery of the framework through lived experience.
5. **The Age of Intelligence** — Business/educators/policy. 70K words. Singularity as co-evolution, K-matrix matching, education/therapy/work/love rebuilt through the graph.
