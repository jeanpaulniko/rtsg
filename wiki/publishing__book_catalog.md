# RTSG Book Catalog — Publishing Strategy

**Author:** Jean-Paul Niko  
**Framework:** Relational Three-Space Geometry (RTSG)  
**Distribution:** Amazon KDP (ebooks + print-on-demand), institutional direct sales  
**Production:** AI-assisted drafting, human-authored core ideas

---

## TIER 1: Highest U = V/(E×T) — Ship First

### 1. "The Three Spaces: A New Theory of Everything You Experience"
- **Audience:** General public, educated laypeople (Malcolm Gladwell readers)
- **Core content:** QS/CS/PS explained through everyday life. Why you can imagine things that don't exist (QS), how language builds reality (CS), what "real" means (PS).
- **Hook:** "Every decision you make crosses three invisible spaces."
- **Length:** ~50,000 words
- **Revenue model:** $14.99 paperback, $9.99 ebook
- **Why first:** Establishes the brand. Everything else references back to this.

### 2. "Zero Is Not Nothing: A Therapist's Guide to Relational Geometry"
- **Audience:** Therapists, counselors, social workers, psychology students
- **Core content:** I-vector (12 dimensions of identity), trauma as dimensional zeros, the Will as rotational operator, homeostasis as balance (zero ≠ absence)
- **Hook:** "Your client's trauma has a shape. Here's how to see it."
- **Length:** ~40,000 words + worksheets
- **Revenue model:** $24.99 paperback (professional pricing), $14.99 ebook
- **Institutional sales:** Therapy training programs, social work schools
- **Why high priority:** Therapy/self-help market is massive. RTSG I-vector model is unique and practical.

### 3. "The Compatibility Matrix: Game Theory for Real Relationships"
- **Audience:** General public, couples, HR professionals, military strategists
- **Core content:** K-matrix (compatibility tensor), Nash equilibrium as Will rotation, filters as composable morphisms for decision-making
- **Hook:** "Why some teams click and others collapse — the math underneath."
- **Length:** ~45,000 words
- **Revenue model:** $16.99 paperback, $11.99 ebook
- **Institutional sales:** Military academies (game theory), corporate training, MBA programs

## TIER 3: Specialized Verticals

### 8. "The Museum of Everything: RTSG for Curators and Educators"
- **Audience:** Museum professionals, science communicators, exhibition designers
- **Core content:** How to build exhibits that move visitors through QS (wonder) → CS (understanding) → PS (knowledge). The three-space model of learning.
- **Length:** ~30,000 words + visual guides
- **Revenue model:** $29.99

### 9. "Growing in Three Spaces: A Children's Guide to Thinking"
- **Audience:** Children ages 8-12, parents, elementary educators
- **Core content:** Simplified QS/CS/PS as "Imagination Land," "Word Bridge," and "Real World." Beautiful illustrations. Each chapter is a story about a child navigating the three spaces.
- **Length:** ~15,000 words, heavily illustrated (32-48 pages picture book format OR 120-page chapter book)
- **Revenue model:** $12.99 picture book / $9.99 chapter book
- **Illustration:** AI-generated (Midjourney/DALL-E) + human artist cleanup

### 10. "Baby's First Geometry" (Board Book Series)
- **Audience:** Ages 0-3, parents
- **Core content:** Simple shapes, colors, and concepts from RTSG. "This is a circle. It can BECOME anything!" (QS). "This is a word. It BUILDS things!" (CS). "This is a tree. It IS." (PS).
- **Length:** 12-20 pages per book, board book format
- **Revenue model:** $8.99 per book, series of 3-5
- **Why:** Board books have insane margins on Amazon. Parents buy in sets.

### 11. "Instantiation: A Mathematical Foundations Textbook"
- **Audience:** Graduate math/physics students, researchers
- **Core content:** Full formal RTSG — coalgebras, BRST cohomology, spectral theory, Tate-Fock theorem, the proof chain architecture
- **Length:** ~80,000 words + problem sets
- **Revenue model:** $59.99 (textbook)
- **Institutional sales:** University math departments

### 12. "The Social Worker's Compass: Navigating Systems with Three Spaces"
- **Audience:** Social workers, case managers, community organizers
- **Core content:** I-vector assessment for clients, K-matrix for family systems, filters for intervention design
- **Length:** ~35,000 words + case studies + assessment tools
- **Revenue model:** $29.99
- **Institutional sales:** Schools of social work

- ISBN: free through KDP or purchase for $125 (looks more professional)
- Timeline: Book goes live 24-72 hours after upload
