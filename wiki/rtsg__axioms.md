## Axiom 0 — Self-Foundation via Relational Primacy *(new in v6.0)*

*Only relational reality admits absolutes and infinities.*

The ambient set theory of RTSG is **ZFA**: Zermelo–Fraenkel with Choice and Aczel's Anti-Foundation Axiom (1988), replacing the standard Axiom of Foundation. This permits self-containing sets and circular structures — necessary for modeling consciousness (which contains models of itself) and the RTSG source space (which contains its own definition).

**Corollaries:**
- In physical space P, all quantities are bounded by measurement and substrate constraints
- In relational space ℛ, no such bounds apply — infinities and absolutes are properties of *structures of relations*
- The Cantor diagonal argument is a theorem about relational structure, not physical objects
- Von Neumann entropy S = −Tr(ρ ln ρ) confirms this: entropy lives in the relation-matrix ρ, not the states

## Axiom 1 — Co-Primordial Thesis

Quantum space QS, physical space PS, and the CS operator CS are co-primordial: all three arise at the Big Bang simultaneously. Neither reduces to the other.

## Axiom 2 — Instantiation

Consciousness entangles with quantum space QS to produce moments of physical reality PS. Each instantiation event converts some QS → PS. Physical reality = the running total of all instantiation events since the Big Bang.

## Axiom 3 — Relations First-Class

A relation between two nodes has the same ontological status as the nodes themselves. Confirmed by: Von Neumann entropy (entropy in ρ, not states), gauge theory (observables invariant under relation transformation), quantum mechanics (entanglement is relational, not local).

Any cognitive system is representable as a vector **I** ∈ ℝⁿ⁽ᵉ⁾ (n=12 for humans, variable per entity) in the variable-dimensional intelligence space:

$$\mathbf{I} = [I_L, I_M, I_S, I_K, I_N, I_A, I_P, I_{IE}]$$

L=Linguistic · M=Mathematical/Logical · S=Spatial · K=Kinesthetic · N=Naturalistic · A=Abstract/Algorithmic · P=Personal/Interpersonal · IE=Interoceptive/Emotional

## Axiom 5 — The SDE Update Loop

All cognitive dynamics follow the stochastic differential equation:
$$dw = \mu(w,t)\,dt + \sigma(w,t)\sqrt{dt}\cdot\mathcal{N}(0,1)$$
with drift μ(w,t) = α(U_target − w) and noise σ(w,t) = β√(w(1−w)).

## Axiom 6 — The Three-Phase Will Principle

$$\sigma dW \;\longrightarrow\; dw = \mu\,dt + \sigma\,dW \;\longrightarrow\; \lambda < 0$$

Blind will → directed will → realized will. The bifurcation where λ crosses zero = the Schopenhauer-Nietzsche Transition = origin of all intention.

## Axiom 7 — Structural Imperfection

Every instantiated system has irreducible structural imperfection: the Conceptual Irreversibility Theorem (CIT) prevents any finite cognitive system from having complete self-knowledge. Perspectival incompleteness is not a bug — it is the engine of drive.

## Axiom 8 — The Drive Principle

Every cognitive system has a Drive **D** = variational principle pushing it away from equilibrium toward complexification. At cosmic scale, the P-projection of **D** = dark energy. The cosmological constant Λ is not a free parameter — it is the magnitude of **D** at cosmic scale.

## Axiom 9 — The Utility Function

All cognitive routing optimizes:
$$U = \frac{\text{value}}{\text{energy} \times \text{time}}$$
