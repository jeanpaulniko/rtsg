# TRIMODAL-EX

**Federated Autonomous Drone System for Extreme Environments**

A complete federated autonomous drone system featuring 6-8 legs plus coconut crab claws with quantum-classical hybrid locomotion planning. The project includes comprehensive materials engineering, cost optimization, and fault-tolerant computing across 11 federated nodes.
