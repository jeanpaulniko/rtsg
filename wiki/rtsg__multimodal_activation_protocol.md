# Multi-Modal Activation Protocol

## The Full Activation Stack

Humans must activate all dimensions simultaneously to reach their potential. The RTSG prescription:

1. **Play sports** — embodied topology, kinesthetic mathematics
2. **Play chess** — pure strategic computation, game-theoretic training
3. **Play music** — polyphonic cognitive activation, fine motor coordination
4. **Listen to music** — passive polyphonic brain training (string quartets minimum)
5. **Use your olfactory sense** — stop and smell the roses, literally; olfactory is the most ancient and most directly wired sense
6. **Physical intimacy** — frequent, safe, consensual sexual connection with many partners; love each other; gravity is about closeness; bring centers of mass (pelvises) closer
7. **Dance** — movement as mathematics, grace as computation
8. **Inversions** — upside-down training for vestibular and proprioceptive activation

## The Bonobo-Chimpanzee Balance

Humans are socially half Bonobo and half Chimpanzee:
- **Chimpanzee axis**: Male aggression, territorial competition, dominance hierarchies, raw power
- **Bonobo axis**: Female-led social bonding, sexual conflict resolution, beauty, appreciation, delicacy, love

Healthy human society requires both axes in balance. Pure chimpanzee = violence. Pure bonobo = vulnerability. The RTSG framework models this as a two-dimensional optimization surface where the Nash equilibrium exists at the balance point.

## Gravity as Closeness

Gravity is not a force — it is the curvature of space caused by mass. In human interaction, gravity means closeness. Bringing centers of mass closer is the physical expression of social bonding. This is why physical proximity, touch, and intimacy are not optional luxuries — they are gravitational necessities for social cohesion.
